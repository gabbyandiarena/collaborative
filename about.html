Our story

Built by dancers, for dancers.

The Collaborative was founded in 2023 with the mission and goal of giving Utah elevated training opportunities to reach their goals. It has become a community of pushing limits and becoming inspired in the room.

Owner & Founder

Erika Cox

Erika founded The Collaborative with the goal of providing a space for any and all dancers to get the most out of elevated & efficient training.

Erika has been involved in the dance community for the majority of her life and has a huge passion for mentoring dancers. She attended Brigham Young University, where she was a member of the BYU Cougarette Dance Team for 3 years — winning 4 National Titles and leading as a Captain for 2 of those years. Erika was given the opportunity to assistant coach The Cougarettes for 2 years while finishing college. She then became the Head Coach of the Bingham Minerette High School Drill Team, coaching there for 7 years — her accolades include 4 consecutive 6A state titles, 7 Region titles, the ICU Worlds Gold Medal, several national titles, and Coach of the Year.

She believes that great training comes from the combination of high standards, passion, and a supportive community. That is where The Collaborative began!
